Delete Your Account

Rupevo AI by NexStream Studios

You can delete your Rupevo AI account and all associated data at any time directly from within the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Rupevo AI app on your device
  2. Go to Profile (tap the profile icon in the bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently and immediately deleted from our servers:

  • Your profile information (name, email)
  • All financial transactions (income and expenses)
  • Budgets and budget history
  • Savings goals
  • EMI schedules and records
  • AI chat history and AI usage records
  • App preferences and settings
  • Subscription and usage data

Warning: Account deletion is irreversible. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please export any financial reports you need before deleting your account.

Data Retention

  • All user data is deleted immediately upon account deletion.
  • Crash report data in Firebase Crashlytics (which does not contain personal financial data) is retained for up to 90 days.
  • No other data is retained after deletion.
